7. Risk Mitigation & Safety
Since we are anonymous, safety is our top priority to comply with Malaysian laws (MCMC).
 * No Login, But Traceable: We track unique Device IDs (Hardware UUID).
 * The "Kill Switch":
   * Harassment: If a user is reported, their Device ID is banned instantly.
   * Scams: If a paid call ends in <60 seconds, we auto-refund the payer to disincentivize scammers.
 * Legal Shield: Mandatory "Agree & Enter" gate where users confirm they are 18+ and acknowledge that illegal acts will be reported to PDRM.
